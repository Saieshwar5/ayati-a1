**Story Outline**

**Setting:** A small, enchanted forest where the trees whisper secrets and magical creatures roam.

**Main Character:** Lily, a curious young girl with a love for adventure and a knack for finding hidden paths.

**Central Event:** One day, while exploring the forest, Lily discovers a shimmering pond that holds ancient magic, revealing visions of her future.

**Beginning:** Lily decides to venture into the enchanted forest, drawn by the tales of its wonders.

**Middle:** As she explores, she encounters various magical creatures who share their wisdom, guiding her to the pond. When she gazes into the pond, she sees a glimpse of a great adventure awaiting her.

**End:** Inspired by the visions, Lily leaves the forest with a sense of purpose, ready to embrace the adventure that lies ahead.
